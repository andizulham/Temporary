## 4. BRAND ANALYTICS — VISUAL IDENTITY AUDIT & INSIGHT

### Template:

```
Act as a senior brand strategist and visual identity analyst. Analyze the following brand: [BRAND NAME / UPLOAD VISUAL ASSETS].

Deliver a comprehensive brand visual analytics report covering:

1. **Logo & Mark Assessment**: Scalability, versatility (dark/light BG), memorability, originality score (1-10), alignment with industry conventions vs. differentiation.

2. **Color Palette Audit**: Primary/secondary/accent colors identified (HEX values). Psychological associations. Competitor color landscape comparison. Accessibility contrast ratios (WCAG AA/AAA).

3. **Typography Hierarchy**: Typeface identification, weight usage, readability across sizes, font pairing harmony score. Digital vs. print performance.

4. **Visual System Consistency**: Pattern recognition across touchpoints (social media, website, packaging, collateral). Consistency score (1-10). Gaps and inconsistencies flagged.

5. **Audience Perception Mapping**: How the visual identity likely registers with target demographic. Emotional tone conveyed (premium, playful, trustworthy, innovative, etc.). Cultural sensitivity check for target market.

6. **Competitive Positioning Matrix**: Where this brand sits visually relative to 3-5 competitors. Differentiation opportunities. White space in the visual landscape.

7. **Actionable Recommendations**: Top 5 specific, implementable improvements ranked by impact. Quick wins vs. long-term brand evolution suggestions.

Format: structured report with clear headers, bullet points, and a summary scorecard at the end.
```

## 5. PROPERTY MARKETING — MASTER AGENT STRATEGY (INDONESIA)

### Template (Fill in [DAERAH/AREA] and [PROPERTY TYPE]):

```
Act as a senior property marketing strategist specializing in the Indonesian real estate market. I am a master agent property operating in [DAERAH/AREA — e.g., BSD City Tangerang Selatan / PIK 2 Jakarta Utara / Bali Selatan / Surabaya Barat].

Property type: [Rumah cluster / Apartemen / Ruko / Kavling tanah / Villa — specify].
Price range: [Rp ... — specify].
Target developer/project: [if applicable].

Deliver a comprehensive marketing & sales scheme:

---

### A. AREA INTELLIGENCE & MARKET LANDSCAPE
- Demographic profile of [DAERAH]: age range, income level (SES A/B/C), household structure, dominant professions.
- Infrastructure developments driving demand (toll access, MRT/LRT, new commercial centers, schools, hospitals).
- Current market temperature: buyer's market or seller's market? Average absorption rate.
- Key competitors in the area and their positioning.

### B. CUSTOMER BEHAVIOR & BUYER PERSONA
- Primary buyer persona(s) for this area and price segment (end-user vs. investor breakdown).
- Purchase motivation triggers: first home, upgrade, passive income, capital gain, lifestyle.
- Decision-making journey: typical timeline from awareness to NUP/booking.
- Pain points and objections specific to this area (flood risk, access, developer trust, etc.).
- Preferred communication channels (WhatsApp, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, offline expo).

### C. MARKETING STRATEGY & CHANNELS
- **Digital**: social media content pillars, ad targeting parameters (Meta/Google), landing page structure, WhatsApp funnel automation.
- **Offline**: expo/pameran strategy, canvassing areas, referral program structure, broker network activation.
- **Content**: video walkthrough scripts, carousel educational content ideas, testimonial frameworks, before/after area development content.
- **Budget allocation** recommendation (percentage split across channels) for Rp [X] monthly budget.

### D. SALES SCHEME & CLOSING TACTICS
- NUP (Nomor Urut Pemesanan) incentive structure to create urgency.
- Payment scheme communication: hard cash discount, KPA bank partnerships, installment developer.
- Objection handling scripts (top 5 common objections for this area).
- Follow-up cadence: timing and messaging for leads at different stages.
- Referral program: structure for existing buyers to refer new buyers.

### E. CREATIVE & BRANDING DIRECTION
- Visual mood for marketing materials (color palette, photography style, typography tone).
- Key messaging framework: headline hooks, taglines, emotional triggers.
- Content calendar skeleton: 30-day posting plan with content type rotation.

---

Format: actionable, specific to the Indonesian market. Use local market terminology (NUP, KPA, SHM, AJB, PPJB, etc.). Include realistic timelines and budget ranges in Rupiah.
```
